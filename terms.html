Services Agreement

Chyston Studio's | OFFICIAL POLICY

1. Booking and Retainer

  • Reservation: A signed contract and a non-refundable retainer fee are required to reserve the date and time of the session.
  • Payment Schedule: The remaining balance is due prior to the session date. If final payment is not received, the Studio reserves the right to cancel the booking.

2. Rescheduling and Cancellation

  • Client Cancellation: If the Client cancels, the retainer is forfeited.
  • Rescheduling: Requests to reschedule must be made in advance.
  • Studio Cancellation: In the event of illness or emergency, the Studio will attempt to provide a substitute or offer a full refund.

3. Artistic Style and Delivery

  • Artistic Discretion: The Studio retains creative control over editing and the final selection of images.
  • Raw Files: The Studio will NOT handout the RAW or .CR3/.CR2/.CRW
  • Editing: Standard color correction is included. Advanced retouching may incur additional fees.
  • Timeline: Final galleries will be delivered via digital download within the agreed timeframe.

4. Usage Rights & Intellectual Property

  • Copyright: The Studio retains the copyright to all images created.
  • Social Media: Client agrees to credit the Studio and will not apply filters to finished images.

5. Model Release

The Client grants the Studio the right to use images for promotional purposes, including portfolio display and social media marketing, unless otherwise agreed upon.

6. Cooperation and Conduct

  • Punctuality: Late arrival time is deducted from the total session duration.
  • Environment: The Studio reserves the right to terminate sessions if the environment becomes unsafe or hostile.

7. Limitation of Liability

Damage/Loss: Liability for lost or damaged files is limited to a refund of the amount paid. The Studio is not liable for injury sustained during the session.
